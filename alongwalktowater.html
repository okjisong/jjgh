A Long Walk to Water

Based on a true story by Linda Sue Park

Main Characters

  • Salva Dut - A Sudanese boy fleeing civil war in 1985
  • Nya - A girl in 2008 who walks hours daily to fetch water

Major Themes

  • Survival and perseverance
  • The water crisis in Africa
  • Hope and resilience
  • Impact of war on children

Story Timeline

1985: Salva escapes war and joins refugee groups.

1990s: Salva moves from refugee camps to the US.

2008: Nya walks daily for water. Her village receives a well thanks to Salva’s nonprofit.
